## 15 Learned Italian-Specific Rules

### Grammar & Structure Rules

1. **Preposition + Article Contractions** - Always keep atomic (del, nella, sugli, etc.)
2. **Reflexive Verb Phrases** - Never split pronouns from verbs (mi piace, ti senti)
3. **Modal + Infinitive** - Keep modal verbs with infinitives (devo andare, posso fare)
4. **Essential Phrasal Verbs** - Protect high-frequency patterns (ho bisogno di, sto cercando di)
5. **Compound Tenses** - Keep auxiliaries with participles (ho fatto, sono andato)
6. **Article + Noun Units** - Never separate articles from nouns (il libro, la ragazza)
7. **Idiomatic Expressions** - Preserve cultural idioms (fare finta di, mettersi d'accordo)
8. **Negation Patterns** - Keep "non" with negated verb
9. **Clitic Pronouns** - Handle attached pronouns correctly (dirmelo, farglielo)
